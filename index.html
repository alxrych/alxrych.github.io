ALXRYCH

Sustainable art + inspired by nature


"I am not seeking to replicate nature, only translate its language into tangible textures."

Multidisciplinary artist based in Spain. With a strong focus on simplicity and timelessness, he consciously chooses natural materials, characterized by a certain fragility, to discover an emotional power in the essential rawness of the materials.
The process begins with the elemental: recycled paper and card are transformed by hand into a dense, tactile ground that forms the first narrative layer. On this organic foundation, pigments interact in a deliberate yet unpredictable dialogue.
Layers, subtle transitions and unrepeatable chromatic shifts emerge as chance and intention converge, creating works that invite physical as much as visual engagement.
The pieces offer a contemplative, personal experience. By merging observation with abstraction, they move beyond representation to capture the moods, rhythms and quiet patterns of the natural world, proposing not simply a landscape but a space for sensory memory.
