Sustainable art with recycled paper

Unique works created with natural and recycled materials

Each piece is crafted on handmade recycled paper, meticulously created from reclaimed paper and cardboard remnants without any chemical processing. The paper itself tells a story of renewal and transformation, providing a unique textured backdrop for the artworks.

The watercolor paintings are original and unique, applied with chemical-free paints made from natural pigments. These organic materials interact with the recycled paper in subtle ways, creating soft textures and gentle color variations that cannot be replicated with synthetic alternatives.


Process

路 Collection: gathering paper and cardboard scraps for recycling.
路 Papermaking: creating paper pulp using only water, no chemicals.
路 Painting: applying watercolors made with natural pigments.
路 Drying: air-drying the artwork naturally, without additives.

ALXRYCH is a self-taught artist based in northern Spain, creating abstract works on recycled paper that are inspired by the beauty of nature and the emotions stirred by its landscapes. Through textures, colours, and fluid forms, his paintings aim to capture the feelings that arise when observing forests, rivers, mountains, and the rugged northern coastline.

Each piece invites the viewer to experience the landscape in a personal and emotional way, reflecting the moods, rhythms, and subtle patterns of the natural world. By blending observation with abstraction transforms everyday scenes into evocative expressions of place and feeling.
