Sustainable art with recycled paper

Unique works created with natural and recycled materials

These artworks aren't just painted; they're built from the ground up. We use only recycled paper and cardboard, meticulously transformed by hand into a thick, tactile base, completely free of chemical processing. The very texture of this paper is the artwork's first layer: it tells a story of second chances and genuine sustainability.

The interaction of pigments with this organic base is truly unique. The result is an interplay of layers, subtle blending, and colour variations that are simply impossible to replicate. More than just a piece of art, it's an artwork with a positive impact, offering unparalleled depth and a touch you'll truly feel.

ALXRYCH is a spanish painter whose work focuses on ethereal interpretations of the natural world through abstract painting. His work explores the emotional resonance of scenes from nature through textures, colours, and fluid forms, his paintings aim to capture the feelings that arise when observing forests, rivers, mountains, and the rugged northern coastline.

Each piece invites the viewer to experience the landscape in a personal and emotional way, reflecting the moods, rhythms, and subtle patterns of the natural world. By blending observation with abstraction transforms everyday scenes into evocative expressions of place and feeling.
