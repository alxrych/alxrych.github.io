ABOUT

Alxrych is a self-taught contemporary artist whose abstract work serves as a bridge between the earthly and the intangible.
His artistic practice emerges from an intimate, ongoing exploration of nature, reinterpreted through a minimalist visual language imbued with emotive depth.
His pieces, crafted primarily with delicate watercolors, capture ethereal landscapes that transcend literal representation to evoke personal universes where nostalgia, serenity, and trust intertwine in chromatic harmony.

Inspired by the subtlety of shifting light, the textures of natural elements, and the stillness of open spaces, Alxrych constructs compositions that oscillate between the organic and the dreamlike. His style, defined by fluid strokes and translucent layers, invites introspection, fostering a dialogue between the viewer and emotional memory.
Each work functions as a sensory map, where desaturated tones and ambiguous forms suggest rather than define, creating space for individual projection. With an aesthetic that evokes timelessness, his work has been described as a "poetics of visual silence" highlighting his ability to balance abstraction with subtle narrative.
